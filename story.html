 Joey Lai

My name is Joey Lai and I live in Calgary, AB. The first programming language was HTML when I was in junior high making personal websites on a social network called CalgaryPlanet (which unfortunately no longer exists). It was not until high school where I found my joy of programming, and decided to make a career out of it. I attended SAIT and graduated with an Information Technology Software Development Diploma (with Honors). After working in industry for a year (at CGI), I decided to go back to school to receive higher education. I attended Mount Royal University taking their Bachelor of Computer Information Systems program with a minor in Business (Management). It was also during that time where I joined a company called ITforDev as an independent software developer consultant and started my own incorporation, jlai Consulting Inc.



I have industry experience for many technologies such as Java, C# .NET, javascript/jQuery. I also enjoy discovering new technologies such as Git, and various architectural frameworks such as MVC and Spring. I practice agile methodologies, test driven development, and design patterns. I hope to improve my programming skills and learn more about software architecture to become a leader and mentor in software development.



If you wish to contact me, feel free to drop me an email. You can also find me on LinkedIn and StackOverflow!
